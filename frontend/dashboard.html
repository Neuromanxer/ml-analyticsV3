How to Use

Quick guide to using our ML Analytics platform

1. Upload Your Data

Begin by uploading a CSV file containing your dataset using the Data Input section. Make sure your data is properly formatted with headers.

2. Configure Your Analysis

Specify columns to drop, choose your target column, and select impactful features you want to focus on for your analysis.

3. Select Analysis Method

Choose between regression, classification, clustering, or visualization tools depending on your goals. Each tool provides different insights into your data.

4. Review Results

After processing, examine the model information, performance metrics, and visualizations to gain insights from your data and make informed decisions.
